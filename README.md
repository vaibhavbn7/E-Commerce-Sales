# E-Commerce Sales Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing E-Commerce sales performance using Power BI and SQL Server. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales, profit, quantity sold, profit margin, product performance, regional trends, and shipping preferences. It enables business stakeholders to monitor key metrics and identify opportunities for improving revenue and profitability.

## Problem Statement

E-commerce businesses generate large volumes of sales data across multiple customer segments, product categories, and geographic regions. However, without an interactive reporting solution, it becomes difficult to track sales performance, identify profitable products, monitor regional trends, and evaluate customer purchasing behavior.

The objective of this project is to develop an interactive Power BI dashboard that:

* Tracks key business KPIs such as Sales, Profit, Quantity, and Profit Margin.
* Compares current year performance with previous year performance.
* Analyzes sales across Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office segments.
* Identifies top-performing and underperforming products.
* Evaluates regional and state-wise sales distribution.
* Monitors shipping mode preferences and their contribution to sales.

## Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* MS SQL Server
* DAX
* Power Query
* Data Modeling

## Dashboard Features

* YTD Sales, Profit, Quantity, and Profit Margin KPIs
* Year-over-Year (YoY) performance comparison
* Segment-wise analysis (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)
* Category-wise sales performance
* Top 5 and Bottom 5 products analysis
* Region-wise sales distribution
* State-wise sales map visualization
* Shipping mode analysis
* Interactive filters and drill-down capabilities

## Key Insights

1. The Consumer segment contributes the highest revenue and profit among all customer segments, making it the primary revenue driver.

2. The West region consistently generates the largest share of sales, while South and Central regions present opportunities for business growth.

3. Standard Class shipping accounts for approximately 60% of total orders, indicating strong customer preference for cost-effective shipping options.

4. Office Supplies contributes the highest sales volume, while Furniture and Technology categories show varying profitability across segments.

5. Top-performing products contribute significantly to overall revenue, whereas several low-performing products generate minimal sales and may require pricing or inventory optimization.

6. Although overall profit margin remains above 11%, sales quantity shows a year-over-year decline, highlighting the need for improved customer acquisition and retention strategies.

## Business Impact

The dashboard enables stakeholders to:

* Monitor business performance in real time.
* Identify profitable customer segments and regions.
* Optimize product portfolio decisions.
* Improve inventory and shipping strategies.
* Make data-driven decisions using interactive visual analytics.
